How to play:

Based on game mode you have to guess whether the search volume, fans amount or company revenue on the right is higher or lower than the search volume, fans amount or company revenue on the left, if you think its higher then click "higher", if you think otherwise then click "lower"

Keyboard shortcuts:

Keyboard shortcuts: What it does: Screen:
"Up Arrow" Choose Higher Game
"Down Arrow" Choose Lower Game
"M key" Main menu Game, Fail screen
"R key" Random mode Main menu
"F key" Fans mode Main menu
"C key" Company mode Main menu
"H key" How to play Main menu
"P key" Play again Fail screen
